### **Section 2: Portion Guide (Who eats how much?)**

Since you are cooking for one household, the **food is the same**, but the **plate composition** changes.

#### **1. The Parents (Manjunath & Lakshmi)**

* **Goal:** Weight loss + Blood Sugar Control + Joint Health.
* **The Plate Rule (12:30 PM Brunch):**
* **50% Vegetables:** Fill half the plate with the Palya/Salad/Sabzi.
* **25% Protein:** The Dal/Curry/Eggs.
* **25% Carb:** The Rice/Ragi/Roti. (Max 1 Ragi Ball or 1.5 Rotis or 1 cup Rice).


* **Special Instruction:**
* If the meal is *Low Protein* (like Lemon Rice or Upma), they **MUST** eat 2 Boiled Eggs or a scoop of protein powder.
* **7:30 PM Dinner:** Stick to Soups/Salads. Try to avoid Roti/Rice at night to let the body burn fat while sleeping.



#### **2. Naga Chethan**

* **Goal:** Weight Loss + Muscle Maintenance (Transitioning to Moderate Activity).
* **The Plate Rule:**
* **40% Vegetables.**
* **35% Protein:** He needs the largest portion of Dal/Chickpeas/Soya.
* **25% Carb:** Same rice portion as parents (don't overeat carbs).


* **Special Instruction:**
* The **4:30 PM Snack** is non-negotiable for him. He needs that fuel to prevent overeating at dinner.



#### **3. Niveda**

* **Goal:** Pre-Pregnancy Health + Weight Management.
* **The Plate Rule:**
* **Balanced:** Equal parts Veggies, Protein, and Carbs.


* **Special Instruction:**
* **Fats are friend:** Don't be afraid of Ghee or Coconut in the stew. Good fats are needed for hormone production.
* **Greens:** On days with Palak, Gongura, or Moringa, she should take a second helping of the greens.

### **Section 3: Recipe Instructions & Health Logic**

#### **A. The Morning Tonics (7:00 AM)**

* **Golden Mix (Immunity/Inflammation):** Boil 1 glass water with grated Ginger, Turmeric, crushed Jeera. Strain. Squeeze 1/2 Lemon + pinch of Pepper (Pepper activates Turmeric).
* **Ash Gourd/Celery Juice (Alkaline/Detox):** Blend Ash Gourd (remove skin/seeds) or Celery with water. Strain. Drink fresh. **Benefit:** Reduces body heat and acidity for parents.
* **Green Juice (Folate/Iron):** Blend Spinach, Cucumber, 1/2 Apple, Lemon. Strain. **Benefit:** Critical for Niveda (Folic Acid) and Anemia prevention.

#### **B. The "Protein Shake" (4:30 PM)**

* *Note: This is not a store-bought powder, but your specific recipe.*
* **Ingredients:** Pumpkin seeds, Watermelon seeds, Makhana, Chia seeds, Poppy seeds.
* **Method:** Soak 1 tbsp of each for 1 hour. Blend into a smoothie with water/milk + Dates/Honey.
* **Why?** This provides Zinc (Male fertility/Immunity) and Magnesium (Sleep/Muscle relaxation).

#### **C. Specific Dishes**

* **Moringa Soup:** Boil Moringa leaves with moong dal water, garlic, and cumin. Mash and strain. **Why:** Moringa is a powerhouse of Calcium (Parents' bones) and Iron.
* **Horsegram (Kulthi) Curry:** Pressure cook Horsegram. Mash half of it to thicken gravy. Add spices. **Why:** Horsegram increases body heat and metabolism, aiding weight loss.
* **Ragi Mudde:** Finger millet balls. **Why:** Low Glycemic Index. It releases energy slowly, preventing sugar spikes in parents.
* **Fermented Kimchi/Kanji:** These are probiotics. **Why?** Gut health links directly to weight loss. A healthy gut absorbs nutrients better and reduces bloating.

#### **D. Night Shots (Bedtime Ritual)**

* **Ingredients:** Turmeric, Nutmeg, Poppy seeds, Cinnamon, Dry Ginger, Pepper.
* **Method:** Roast dry spices, grind to powder. Mix 1/4 tsp in warm water/milk.
* **Why?** Nutmeg and Poppy seeds are natural sleep aids. Turmeric heals inflammation from the day's activity.

### **Final "Single Kitchen" Prep Tips**

1. **Weekend:** Grind your Idli/Dosa batter and **Moong Dal batter** (for Pesarattu/Chilla).
2. **Bulk Cook Base:** Make a large batch of "Onion-Tomato Masala" (Bhuna Masala). You can use this for Rajma, Chana, and Egg Curry throughout the week.
3. **Boiled Eggs:** Boil 12-15 eggs at once. Keep them in the fridge (shell on). They last 3-4 days and are instant protein add-ons.
